About BotsDNA

BotsDNA – Learn UiPath the Right Way

At BotsDNA, we focus only on UiPath. Our goal is to help you learn Robotic Process Automation (RPA) through clear explanations, real-time projects, and step-by-step guidance.

BotsDNA was founded by Sudheer Nimmagadda, a 3-time UiPath MVP and 2-time hackathon winner. With over 10,000 YouTube subscribers and many paid learners, BotsDNA has become a trusted place for quality UiPath training.

We are on a mission to train 9,000 people and help them build strong careers in automation.

Why Learn with BotsDNA?

UiPath-Only Training: Focused, deep learning with no distractions.

Hands-On Practice: Learn by building real projects and bots.

Expert Mentorship: Get guidance from Sudheer Nimmagadda himself.

Career-Oriented: Learn what the industry expects in real jobs.

Affordable & Flexible: High-quality training at your own pace.
